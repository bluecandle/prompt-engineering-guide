Mid-Century Inspired Office Chair

Introducing a stunning addition to our mid-century inspired office furniture collection, this office chair is designed to blend seamlessly into both home and business settings. The chair is available in several shell colors and base finishes, allowing for a high degree of customization to match any decor. Choose between a plastic back with front upholstery (SWC-100) or full upholstery (SWC-110) in a variety of 10 fabric and 6 leather options. The base finishes include stainless steel, matte black, gloss white, or chrome, providing a sleek and modern look.

Constructed with a 5-wheel plastic coated aluminum base, this chair ensures durability and ease of movement. The pneumatic chair adjust feature allows for effortless height adjustments. The chair is available with or without armrests, and you can select from soft or hard-floor caster options. Additionally, you have the choice of two seat foam densities: medium (1.8 lb/ft3) or high (2.8 lb/ft3), and armless or 8 position PU armrests.

The shell base glider is made from cast aluminum with a modified nylon PA6/PA66 coating, ensuring a robust and long-lasting structure. The seat is crafted from HD36 foam, providing comfort and support for extended periods of use. With a shell thickness of 10 mm, this chair is built to last. Proudly made in Italy, this chair is also qualified for contract use, making it a versatile choice for various environments.
